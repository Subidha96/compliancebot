================================================================================
Individual Privacy Act 2075 (2018) — Key Provisions Summary
Source: Nepal Law Commission
================================================================================

This document provides an educational summary of the Individual Privacy Act 2075. For official text, refer to the Nepal Gazette.

1. SCOPE AND APPLICATION
   - Applies to all personal data of individuals in Nepal
   - Covers both digital and physical records
   - Applies to government and private sector

2. DEFINITION OF PERSONAL DATA
   - Name, address, contact information
   - Financial information
   - Health records
   - Biometric data
   - Any information that can identify an individual

3. DATA PROCESSING PRINCIPLES
   - Lawfulness, fairness, and transparency
   - Purpose limitation
   - Data minimization
   - Accuracy
   - Storage limitation
   - Integrity and confidentiality

4. INDIVIDUAL RIGHTS
   - Right to access personal data
   - Right to rectification of inaccurate data
   - Right to object to processing
   - Right to data portability (limited)

5. DATA PROTECTION OFFICER
   - Required for organizations processing sensitive data
   - Responsible for compliance monitoring
   - Acts as contact point for data subjects

6. BREACH NOTIFICATION
   - Notify affected individuals within 72 hours
   - Notify Privacy Commission for significant breaches
   - Documentation of breach and remediation

7. PENALTIES
   - Unauthorized access: up to NPR 100,000 fine
   - Data breach due to negligence: up to NPR 500,000
   - Repeat offenses: higher penalties and potential criminal charges
