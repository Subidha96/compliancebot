SYNTHETIC EDUCATIONAL SUMMARY — not official legal text
================================================================================
Electronic Transactions Act 2063 (2006) — Key Clauses Summary
Source: Nepal Law Commission
================================================================================

This document provides an educational summary of the Electronic Transactions Act 2063. For official text, refer to the Nepal Gazette.

1. LEGAL RECOGNITION OF ELECTRONIC RECORDS (Section 3-6)
   - Electronic records and transactions have legal validity
   - Digital signatures are legally recognized
   - Contracts formed electronically are enforceable

2. CYBERCRIME PROVISIONS (Section 7-12)
   - Unauthorized access to computer systems: up to 3 years imprisonment or NPR 30,000 fine
   - Data alteration or destruction: up to 5 years imprisonment
   - Privacy violations: up to 3 years imprisonment or NPR 30,000 fine
   - Identity theft and fraud provisions

3. CERTIFYING AUTHORITIES
   - Licensing of certifying authorities
   - Requirements for digital signature certificates
   - Liability of certifying authorities

4. OFFENCES AND PENALTIES
   - Unauthorized access: 3 years or NPR 30,000
   - Data interference: 5 years or NPR 50,000
   - System interference: 3 years or NPR 30,000
   - Misuse of electronic signature: 2 years or NPR 20,000

5. JURISDICTION
   - Cases can be filed in district courts
   - Nepal Police Cyber Bureau handles investigations

Note: This Act is currently under review for updates to address emerging technologies and align with international standards.
