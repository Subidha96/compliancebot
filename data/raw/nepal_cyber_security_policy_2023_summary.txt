================================================================================
National Cyber Security Policy 2023 — Key Provisions Summary
Source: Nepal Cabinet, August 2023
================================================================================

This document provides an educational summary of Nepal's National Cyber Security Policy 2023. For official text, refer to the Nepal Gazette.

1. ESTABLISHMENT OF NATIONAL CYBER SECURITY CENTER
   - Established under Ministry of Communication and Information Technology (MoCIT)
   - Responsible for national cybersecurity strategy implementation
   - Coordinates across government agencies

2. CRITICAL INFORMATION INFRASTRUCTURE PROTECTION
   - Identifies critical sectors: banking, telecommunications, energy, government services
   - Requires risk assessments for critical infrastructure operators
   - Mandates incident reporting within 24 hours

3. CAPACITY BUILDING
   - National cybersecurity training programs
   - Public awareness campaigns
   - Academic curriculum development in cybersecurity

4. INCIDENT RESPONSE
   - National Computer Emergency Response Team (NP-CERT)
   - Coordination with international CERTs
   - Mandatory breach notification requirements

5. COMPLIANCE FRAMEWORK
   - Sector-specific compliance requirements
   - Regular audits and assessments
   - Penalties for non-compliance

For the complete official text, visit: https://mocit.gov.np
