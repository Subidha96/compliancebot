VERIFIED CONTROL TITLES — ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Annex A (93 controls, 4 themes)
Source: International Organization for Standardization
Official: https://www.iso.org/standard/27001
================================================================================

ISO/IEC 27001:2022 itself is a paid standard; ISO does not publish the full
requirement text of each control for free. The control numbers and titles
below are the actual, complete Annex A control list (publicly listed by ISO's
accredited certification/training partners and consistent with the published
standard structure) — not invented or abbreviated. For the full requirement
text of any control, consult the official purchased standard or an accredited
certification body.

5. COMPANION DOCUMENTS
   - ISO/IEC 27002:2022 provides implementation guidance for each control
   - Certification audits conducted by accredited bodies
   - Three-year certification cycle with annual surveillance audits

Note: ISO 27001:2013 had 114 controls across 14 domains (with "A." prefix,
e.g. A.5.1); ISO 27001:2022 restructured these into 93 controls across 4
themes, dropping the "A." prefix in numbering (e.g. now "5.1" not "A.5.1").
